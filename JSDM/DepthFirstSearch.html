JSDM::DepthFirstSearch

Performs a depth-first search on a DirectedGraph. This class implements the algorithm described on page 541 of Introduction to Algorithms, 2nd Edition by Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, and Stein.

See en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Depth-first_search for a more accessible explanation of what a depth-first search is.
